Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

How can I change my profile picture?

Go to your profile page and hover over your current profile picture. Click the camera icon that appears and select a new image from your device.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the three dots menu on any post or comment and select "Report". Choose the reason for reporting and provide any additional details.

How can I manage my privacy settings?

Go to Settings > Privacy to control who can see your profile, posts, and personal information. You can customize settings for different aspects of your account.
